Mortgage

Chances are that a young person is reading this article, which means you won't have to worry about a mortgage for a while, but it's always good to be aware.

What is it?

A mortgage is a loan, and it is acquired when you want to buy a house. Unless you can pay for a house in full by yourself, which the majority of us will not, you will need to borrow money to buy a house, which is where a mortgage comes in.
