Debt

Debt is something very important to undertand, as it is something everybody is going to have, and it must be handled responsibly.

What is it?

Debt is money you owe someone, usually a financial institution, after you've borrowed money from them to finance purchases. This money also accrues interest, so the unpaid debt grows with time.

Sources of Debt

Credit Cards
Line of Credit
Mortgage
Student Loans

Tips on debt

  • Live within your means. It's easy to convince yourself you need this or need that, but discipline is important in order to remain in good financial standing.
  • Pay monthly bills in full. This helps to avoid interest and keeps your accounts in good standing.
  • Make a plan to pay your debts. Lay out a budget to gain an idea of how much you're paying, and go from there.
